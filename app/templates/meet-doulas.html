Meet the Doulas

A mother–daughter team walking beside you through birth and beyond.

Nicole

Birth has shaped my life in more ways than I can count.

As the mother of nine, I have experienced firsthand the strength, vulnerability, and transformation that pregnancy and birth bring. Each of my own births taught me something new — about surrender, resilience, trust, and the remarkable capacity of a woman’s body. Those experiences ignited in me a deep love for supporting other women as they step into motherhood.

For over twenty-five years, I have walked alongside women in this sacred season — offering encouragement, perspective, and steady presence. As my children grew and began leaving home, I felt called to devote myself more fully to this work. I completed my formal doula training and began Mind, Body, Soul Doula alongside my youngest daughter.

Birth is not simply a physical event — it touches a woman’s mind, her body, and her soul. My heart is to ensure that every woman feels seen, informed, respected, and empowered throughout this profound transition.

Mariah

Growing up in a large family, babies and motherhood were woven into the rhythm of my life. From a young age, I developed a deep appreciation for the beauty and strength found in this season.

As I support women now, I do so with both tenderness and intention — always learning, always listening. I understand that pregnancy and birth can hold both joy and uncertainty, and I value being a steady, reassuring presence during this transformative time.

I am currently training as a full-spectrum doula and have a particular interest in nutrition that strengthens and prepares the body for pregnancy and childbirth. I believe that when a woman feels informed in her mind, supported in her body, and steady in her spirit, she can approach birth with greater peace and confidence.

Two Generations. One Steady Presence.

Together, we offer balanced, thoughtful support — blending lived experience with modern birth knowledge. Our goal is simple: that you feel calm, confident, and deeply supported every step of the way.
